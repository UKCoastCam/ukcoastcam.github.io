About Coastcam.org

Coastcam.org is a free, independent directory of publicly available coastal, harbour and maritime webcams.

The site was created to make it easy to find live views of the coast — whether you're checking weather and sea conditions, planning a trip, keeping an eye on your home waters, or just enjoying the view.

What Coastcam.org Is (and Isn’t)

Coastcam.org does not operate or control any of the webcams listed on this site. All webcams remain the property of their respective owners and operators.

We simply link to publicly available camera feeds and pages provided by:

  • Harbour authorities
  • Sailing clubs and marinas
  • Local councils and organisations
  • Private individuals and businesses

Accuracy and Availability

Webcam availability, image quality and uptime can change without notice. We aim to keep listings accurate, but some links may occasionally stop working or be moved.

If you spot a broken link or an incorrect location, we’d really appreciate you letting us know via the report a fault button on the main page.

Submitting a Webcam

If you run a coastal or maritime webcam and would like it included, or if you know of a publicly accessible webcam that isn’t listed yet, you can submit it using the Submit a coastal webcam button on the main page.

All submissions are reviewed before being added.

Why the Site Is Free

Coastcam.org is run as a personal project. Any advertising shown helps cover basic running costs such as domain registration and site maintenance.

We aim to keep advertising minimal and unobtrusive.
