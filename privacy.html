Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Coastcam.org is a free directory of publicly available coastal, harbour and maritime webcams. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers directly from visitors.

If you submit a webcam via a form, the information provided (such as webcam URL and location) is used solely for the purpose of reviewing and adding webcams to the site.

Cookies and Analytics

Coastcam.org may use cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and understand how the site is used. This may include:

  • Basic analytics (such as page views and approximate location)
  • Advertising cookies used by third-party vendors

Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from partner sites here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Third-Party Websites

Coastcam.org links to external webcam websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some site features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
